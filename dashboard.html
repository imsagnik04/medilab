Equipment Available

PPE KITS

Personal protective equipment is protective clothing, helmets, goggles, or other garments or equipment designed to protect the wearer's body from injury or infection.

Respirators

A respirator is a device designed to protect the wearer from inhaling hazardous atmospheres, including fumes, vapours, gases and particulate matter such as dusts and airborne microorganisms

Diagnostic test kits

Rapid tests are designed for use where a preliminary screening test result is required and are especially useful in resource-limited settings.

Face shields

A face shield, an item of personal protective equipment, aims to protect the wearer's entire face from hazards such as flying objects and road debris, chemical splashes, or potentially infectious materials.

Ambulances

An ambulance is a medically equipped vehicle which transports patients to treatment facilities, such as hospitals.

Gloves

A glove is a garment covering the hand. Gloves usually have separate sheaths or openings for each finger and the thumb.
